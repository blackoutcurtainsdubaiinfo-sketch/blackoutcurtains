What Are Blackout Curtains?

Blackout curtains are specially designed window coverings made with thick fabric or lining that blocks up to 99% of external light. Unlike regular curtains, they are perfect for bedrooms (for undisturbed sleep), living rooms (for cozy ambiance), offices (for glare-free workspaces), and home theaters (for cinema-like experiences).

They are available in different designs, colors, and fabrics to match your interior décor perfectly.
